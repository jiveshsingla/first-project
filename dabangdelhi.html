Dabang Delhi (DBD) is a Kabaddi club based in New Delhi, India that plays in the Pro Kabaddi League. The team is currently led by Meraj Sheykh and coached by Krishan Kumar Hooda. The team is owned by Radha Kapoor and they play their home matches at the Thyagaraj Sports Complex, New Delhi.Pro Kabaddi League (PKL) is a professional Kabaddi league in India, based on the format of the Indian Premier League T20 cricket tournament.
The first edition of the tournament was played in 2014 with eight franchises representing various cities in India. Dabang Delhi is a Delhi based franchise owned by Radha Kapoor.It has a dedicated following in New York City, NY and Tampa Bay, FL.
