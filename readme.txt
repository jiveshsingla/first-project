Project Name: - Pro Kabaddi
Why Pro Kabaddi?
Kabaddi is a sport which most of us played in our lives. Mostly in the rural areas this sport is very popular among the youth as well as the adults. It is our cultural sport.
Aim: -
Our website provides basic information of Indian Kabaddi Teams in an organized manner for everyone who want to know about this sport. Its is basically for people who play Kabaddi or are fans of Kabaddi Sport. It provides information on Kabaddi Teams and their achievements. It also provides information about each player like their name, nation, birthdate and position.
