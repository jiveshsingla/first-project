HARYANA STEALERS

Haryana Steelers (HAR) is a Kabaddi team based in Sonipat, Haryana that plays in the Pro Kabaddi League. The team is currently led by Surender Nada and coached by Ranbir Singh Khokhar. The team is owned by JSW Sports, part of the Jindal South West (JSW) Group. The Steelers play their home matches at Motilal Nehru School of Sports, Rai.
The first edition of the tournament was played in 2014 with eight franchises representing various cities in India. Hryana Stealers is a Haryana based franchise owned by Radha Kapoor.It has a dedicated following in New York City, NY and Tampa Bay, FL.
